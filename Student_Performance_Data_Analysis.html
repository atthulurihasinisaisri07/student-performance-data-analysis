Conclustion

In this project, student performance data was analyzed using Python. The dataset contained 1000 student records with academic scores and background information. Data exploration and visualization revealed that students performed best in reading, while math was the most challenging subject. Gender-wise differences in performance were minimal. Students who completed the test preparation course scored significantly higher across all subjects, indicating the positive impact of structured preparation on academic outcomes.
